# Settings

The Settings page lets you manage your personal profile, account preferences, and team members.

![Settings page](/images/screenshots/settings-profile.png)

## Accessing Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar to open the settings page. You'll see tabs at the top for different sections:

- **Profile** -- Your personal information and password
- **Account** -- Account preferences
- **Members** -- Team member management (administrators only)

## Profile Settings

### Personal Information

Your profile shows your basic information:
- **First Name** -- Your first name
- **Last Name** -- Your last name
- **Email Address** -- The email associated with your account (read-only)

To edit your name:
1. Click the **Edit** button in the Personal Information section
2. Update your first or last name
3. Save your changes

> **Note:** Your email address cannot be changed from the settings page. Contact admin@bluecharter.fr if you need to update your email.

### Change Password

To change your password:
1. In the Profile section, find the **Change Password** area
2. Click **"Send password reset email to your registered email address"**
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Click the link and set your new password

### My Companies

The "My Companies" section shows which companies you have access to:
- **Administrators** see a note indicating they have full access to all companies in the system, with a link to **Manage Members**
- **Regular users** see the list of companies they've been assigned to

## Account Settings

The Account tab contains additional account preferences and settings.

## Members (Administrators Only)

If you're an administrator, the **Members** tab lets you manage team members:

- View all users in the system
- Invite new members to join a company
- Edit user roles and permissions
- Remove users from the system

### Inviting a New Member

1. Go to **Settings > Members**
2. Click the invite/add member button
3. Enter the user's email address
4. Select the company they should be assigned to
5. Choose their role (admin or user)
6. Send the invitation

The new member will receive an email invitation to set up their account.

## Need Help?

If you have any issues with your settings or account, contact **admin@bluecharter.fr** for assistance.
