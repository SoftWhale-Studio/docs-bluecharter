# Settings

The Settings page lets you manage your personal profile and account preferences.

![Settings page](/images/screenshots/settings-profile.png)

## Accessing Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar to open the settings page. You'll see tabs at the top for different sections:

- **Profile** -- Your personal information, password, and company access
- **Account** -- Account preferences

## Profile Settings

### Personal Information

Your profile shows your basic information:
- **First Name** -- Your first name
- **Last Name** -- Your last name
- **Email Address** -- The email associated with your account (read-only)

To edit your name:
1. Click the **Edit** button in the Personal Information section
2. Update your first or last name
3. Save your changes

> **Note:** Your email address cannot be changed from the settings page. Contact admin@bluecharter.fr if you need to update your email.

### Change Password

To change your password:
1. In the Profile section, find the **Change Password** area
2. Click **"Send password reset email to your registered email address"**
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Click the link and set your new password

### My Companies

The "My Companies" section shows which companies you have access to. You'll see:
- The name of each company you've been assigned to
- When you became a member of that company

If you need access to additional companies, contact your administrator at **admin@bluecharter.fr**.

## Account Settings

The Account tab contains additional account preferences and settings.

## Need Help?

If you have any issues with your settings or account, contact **admin@bluecharter.fr** for assistance.
