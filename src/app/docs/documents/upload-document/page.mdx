# My Uploads

The **My Uploads** page is where you submit new documents for admin review. You can upload files, select which company they belong to, and track the status of your previous uploads.

![My Uploads page](/images/screenshots/my-uploads.png)

## How to Upload a Document

1. Click **My Uploads** in the sidebar
2. Select the **company** this document belongs to from the dropdown
3. Drag and drop your file onto the upload area, or click **Browse Files** to select a file from your computer
4. The document will be uploaded and submitted for admin review

## Upload Area

The upload section includes:

- **Company selector** -- Choose which company this document is for. You'll see all companies you have access to.
- **File drop zone** -- Drag and drop a file, or click to browse your computer
- **File requirements** -- Accepted formats include PDF, Word documents, and images. Maximum file size is **10 MB**.

## After Uploading

Once you upload a document:

- It appears in the **My Uploads** list at the bottom of the page
- An administrator will review the document, categorize it, and associate it with the correct yacht
- Once approved, the document will appear in the main **Documents** section

## Upload History

Below the upload area, you'll see a list of all documents you've previously uploaded. This lets you track:

- Which documents you've submitted
- When they were uploaded
- Their current status

## Tips

- Make sure the file is not larger than **10 MB**
- Select the correct company before uploading
- Upload one document at a time for best results
- If you need to upload documents for multiple companies, change the company selector between uploads
