# Email Notifications

BlueCharter automatically notifies you by email when new documents are added to your company.

## Daily Document Digest

Instead of receiving one email per document, you get a single daily digest email — sent each day at **18:00 CEST** — that summarises all new documents uploaded that day.

The email includes:

- The number of new documents available
- Your company name
- A direct link to view those documents

Only company users receive this email. Administrators do not.

## Viewing New Documents from the Email

Clicking the link in the digest email opens the Documents page pre-filtered to show **only the documents that were just added** — you won't see older documents already in your library.

To go back to seeing all documents, clear the filter by refreshing the page or removing the active date filter.

## When Is the Email Sent?

The digest is sent once per day at **18:00 CEST**, but only if new documents were uploaded that day. If nothing was added, no email is sent.

| Scenario | Result |
|---|---|
| New documents uploaded during the day | Digest email sent at 18:00 CEST |
| No new documents uploaded | No email sent |

## Not Receiving Emails?

If you are not receiving digest emails:

- Check your spam or junk folder — the first email may be filtered
- Make sure your account email address is correct in [Settings](/docs/settings)
- Contact **admin@bluecharter.fr** if the issue persists
