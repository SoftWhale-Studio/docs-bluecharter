# Dashboard

The dashboard is the first page you see after logging in. It gives you a quick overview of your yacht management activity.

![Dashboard](/images/screenshots/dashboard.png)

## Overview Cards

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see three summary cards:

### Active Yachts
Shows the number of yachts currently active in the system. Click **View All** to go to the Yachts page filtered by active status.

### Total Clients / Total Documents
- **Administrators** see the total number of clients (companies) in the system
- **Regular users** see the total number of documents they have access to

Click **View All** to navigate to the full list.

### Unpaid Invoices
Shows how many invoices have a "Not Paid" status. This is a quick way to see outstanding payments. Click **View All** to go to the Invoices page filtered to show only unpaid invoices.

## Recent Activity

Below the summary cards, you'll find two sections:

### Recent Documents
Shows the 4 most recently uploaded documents, including:
- Document name
- Associated company and yacht
- Upload date

Click **View All** to see the full documents list.

### Recent Invoices
Shows the 4 most recent invoices, including:
- Invoice number
- Amount
- Client name
- How long ago it was added

Click **View All** to see the full invoices list.

## Dark Mode

You can toggle between light and dark mode using the button in the top-right corner of the navigation bar. Your preference is saved for future sessions.

## Data Sync

In the top navigation bar, you'll see the **Last sync** timestamp showing when your data was last synchronized with the accounting system (Odoo). You can also click the **Sync** button to trigger a manual sync.
