# Getting Started

Welcome to **BlueCharter** (myBlue). This guide will walk you through everything you need to get up and running with the platform.

## What is BlueCharter?

BlueCharter is a document and invoice management platform built for yacht charter companies. It centralizes your fleet documentation, tracks invoices from your accounting system, and provides a clear overview of your operations.

### Key features

- **Document Management** -- View, download, and organize documents for each yacht in your fleet
- **Document Uploads** -- Submit new documents for admin review through the My Uploads page
- **Invoice Tracking** -- View invoices synced from your accounting system, filter by payment status, and download PDFs
- **Dashboard Overview** -- See your active yachts, unpaid invoices, and recent activity at a glance
- **Company-Based Access** -- You see only the yachts, documents, and invoices for the companies you've been assigned to

## Your Dashboard

Once you log in, you land on your personalized dashboard.

![Dashboard overview](/images/screenshots/dashboard.png)

The dashboard shows:

- **Active Yachts** -- How many yachts are currently active across your companies
- **Total Documents** -- A quick count of documents you have access to
- **Unpaid Invoices** -- The number of invoices that still need to be paid
- **Recent Documents** -- The latest documents uploaded to the system
- **Recent Invoices** -- The most recent invoices from your accounting system

Click **View All** on any section to jump directly to the full list.

## Navigation

The sidebar on the left gives you access to all sections of the app:

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Your overview and quick stats |
| **Yachts** | Your fleet of yachts |
| **Documents** | All yacht-related documents |
| **My Uploads** | Upload new documents for review |
| **Invoices** | Invoices synced from your accounting system |
| **Settings** | Your profile and account preferences |
