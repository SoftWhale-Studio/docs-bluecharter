# Signing In

Once your account has been set up, you can sign in to BlueCharter at any time.

## How to Sign In

1. Go to **[myblue.bluecharter.fr](https://myblue.bluecharter.fr)**
2. Enter your **email address**
3. Enter your **password**
4. Click **Login**

![Login page](/images/screenshots/login-page.png)

You'll be redirected to your dashboard after a successful login.

## Forgot Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it:

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot your password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your email inbox for the reset link
5. Click the link and set a new password
6. Log in with your new password

> **Tip:** If you don't see the reset email, check your spam or junk folder.

## Password Requirements

When setting or resetting your password, make sure it:
- Is at least **8 characters** long
- Includes a mix of character types (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols)
- Passes the password strength indicator shown on screen

## Staying Signed In

BlueCharter keeps your session active for a period of time. If your session expires, you'll be redirected to the login page and will need to sign in again.

## Having Trouble?

If you can't sign in, try these steps:

1. **Double-check your email** -- Make sure you're using the same email that was invited
2. **Reset your password** -- Use the "Forgot your password?" link
3. **Clear your browser cache** -- Sometimes old data can cause issues
4. **Contact support** -- Email admin@bluecharter.fr for assistance
