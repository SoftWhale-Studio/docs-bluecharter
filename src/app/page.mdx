**BlueCharter** is a document and invoice management platform designed for yacht charter companies. It helps you manage your fleet documentation, track invoices, and streamline your administrative workflows.

---

## What can you do with BlueCharter?

### Manage Documents
Upload, review, and organize documents for your yachts. Track document status through the approval workflow. [Learn more &rarr;](/docs/documents)

### Track Invoices
View and filter invoices synced from your accounting system. Monitor outstanding payments at a glance. [Learn more &rarr;](/docs/invoices)

### Configure Settings
Customize your profile, manage preferences, and configure your account settings. [Learn more &rarr;](/docs/settings)
