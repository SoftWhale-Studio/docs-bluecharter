**BlueCharter** is a document and invoice management platform designed for yacht charter companies. It helps you manage your fleet documentation, track invoices, and streamline your administrative workflows.

---

## What can you do with BlueCharter?

### View Your Yachts
Browse the yachts assigned to your company. See yacht details, status, and associated documents at a glance.

### Manage Documents
View, download, and organize documents for your yachts. Search by category, company, or yacht. [Learn more &rarr;](/docs/documents)

### Upload Documents
Submit new documents for admin review through the My Uploads page. [Learn more &rarr;](/docs/documents/upload-document)

### Track Invoices
View invoices synced from your accounting system. Filter by payment status to quickly find outstanding payments. [Learn more &rarr;](/docs/invoices)

### Configure Settings
Update your profile, change your password, and see which companies you have access to. [Learn more &rarr;](/docs/settings)
